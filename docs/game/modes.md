# Game Modes

AIRMASH has three game modes. Each plays quite differently.

::: tip Want to play?
Head to the [Where to Play](/where-to-play) page to jump into a game.
:::

---

## Free For All (FFA)

<div class="mode-card">

Every pilot for themselves. Kill to earn score, die and lose some. Climb the leaderboard.

**How it works:**
- You spawn, you fight, you respawn. No teams, no objectives beyond the kill.
- Score is gained from kills and lost on death — the leaderboard reflects your current streak.
- Inferno and shield powerups spawn around the map.
- Upgrades drop when you kill players with enough score.

**Map:** The full world map with regional spawn zones (Europe, Canada, Latin America, Asia).

</div>

---

## Capture the Flag (CTF)

<div class="mode-card">

Two teams. Two flags. Grab theirs, defend yours.

**How it works:**
- Blue and Red teams each have a base with a flag.
- Capture the enemy flag by flying it back to your own base while your flag is safe.
- Your team's base has a periodic shield that protects the flag area.
- Respawn near your base after dying.

**Strategy:**
- Coordinate between attack and defense.
- Mohawks and Predators are great runners — fast enough to grab and go.
- Goliaths can guard the base and repel attackers.
- Prowlers can stealth-grab flags.
- In Air-Battle, bots strategize between `#capture` and `#defend` and will pass the flag to teammates.

**Map:** The full world map with bases at opposite ends.

This is the mode Air-Battle hosts — [play it now](https://game.air-battle.net).

</div>

---

## Battle Royale (BTR)

<div class="mode-card">

Last one flying wins. A shrinking firewall forces everyone together.

**How it works:**
- Players gather in a waiting area until enough join.
- When the match starts, everyone is placed on the map.
- A firewall closes in from the edges over time — stay inside or take damage.
- No respawns. Last player alive wins the round.
- After the round ends, the next match begins after a short wait.

**Strategy:**
- Positioning matters — don't get pushed to the edge of the firewall.
- Collect powerups early while the map is large.
- Predator boost is great for escaping tight spots.

**Map:** The standard map, with a visible boundary that shrinks toward the center.

</div>
